About Us

ABOUTUS

We are all about creating images never seen before images by taking a departure from the conventionality in the industry.

OUR VISION

We ask our selves, how can we make people see the world differently? It is with this passion we run with a vision to redefine and tell stories that matter, stories that affect our society and culture.

We are set to pioneer the implementation of existing innovative technologies to the African subtext, technologies like visual effects, 3D, virtual reality among others.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to be at the forefront of inventing and adopting innovative technologies for visual storytelling. We are on a mission to export the ingenuity of the African culture to the rest of the world via the medium of film.

We want to be a global reference point, our dream is simple; someday in a Chinese and American cinema, there would be long queues to see a made in Africa film.

A ripple effect of establishing a multi million dollar VFX industry is the tremendous value to the economy, think of the number of jobs we can create and the jobs we are going to bring to our studios.

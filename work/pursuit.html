Short Film

Pursuit

View on Youtube

Pursuit is a short action film set in Lagos Nigeria. The film addresses the issue of crime in our society in a tactical manner, thus the tagline “No reason to commit crime”.

About the Production

The film is set to re-orient the perception of Nigeria abroad and boost tourism via our beautiful chase sequences set in the backdrop of“Eko” CMS Marina that showcases our modern respondence systems like the cool RRS police cars & well-equipped ambulance by LASAMBUS .
